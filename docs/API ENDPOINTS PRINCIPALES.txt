API: ENDPOINTS PRINCIPALES (REST)
Base URL: /api/v1
Módulo: Autenticación & Usuario
1. POST /auth/register (Usuario final)
o Request: {email, password, name, phone?}
o Response: {user: {id, name, email}, token}
2. POST /auth/register-business (Negocio)
o Request: {business_name, tax_id, owner_email, owner_name, phone, ...}
o Response: {message: "Pending approval", business_id}
3. POST /auth/login (Todos)
o Request: {email, password, guard: 'user'|'business'|'admin'}
o Response: {user, token}
4. POST /auth/logout (Todos) [Token required]
5. POST /auth/forgot-password (Todos)
6. GET /user/profile [Token required]
o Response: {id, name, email, phone, avatar_url, appointments_count}

MÓDULO: NEGOCIO PÚBLICO (APP)
7. GET /businesses (Listado con filtros)
• Query Params: ?category=hair&near=lat,lng&search=Barber
• Response: {data: [{id, name, category, avg_rating, logo_url, ...}]}
8. GET /businesses/{id} (Detalle)
• Response: {id, name, description, locations: [...], services: [...], ...}
9. GET /businesses/{id}/services (Servicios de un negocio)
MÓDULO: DISPONIBILIDAD Y RESERVAS (APP)
10. GET /availability/slots (Genera slots)
• Query Params: ?business_id=X&service_id=Y&location_id=Z&date=2023-10-
27&employee_id?=A
• Response: {slots: [{start_time, end_time, employee_id, employee_name}]}
11. POST /appointments (Crear cita) [Token user required]
• Request: {business_id, service_id, location_id, employee_id?, datetime, client_notes?}
• Response: {appointment: {id, code, datetime, status}}
12. GET /user/appointments (Mis citas) [Token user required]
• Response: {upcoming: [...], past: [...]}
13. PATCH /appointments/{id}/cancel (Cancelar cita) [Token user required]

MÓDULO: PANEL DE NEGOCIO (GESTIÓN)
14. GET /business/dashboard (Métricas) [Token business required, Scope: admin/manager]
• Response: {today_appointments, revenue, occupancy_rate}
15. GET /business/locations (Listar sucursales) [Token business required]
16. POST /business/locations (Crear sucursal) [Token business required, Permission:
manage_locations]
17. GET /business/services (Listar servicios) [Token business required]
18. POST /business/services (Crear servicio) [Token business required, Permission:
manage_services]
19. GET /business/employees (Listar empleados) [Token business required]
20. POST /business/employees (Crear empleado) [Token business required, Permission:
manage_employees]
21. GET /business/appointments (Listar citas del negocio) [Token business required]
• Query Params: ?location_id=X&date=2023-10-27&status=confirmed
22. PATCH /business/appointments/{id} (Actualizar cita - estado) [Token business required,
Permission: manage_appointments]
• Request: {status: "completed"}

Módulo: Administración de Plataforma
23. GET /admin/businesses/pending [Token admin required]
24. POST /admin/businesses/{id}/approve [Token admin required]
25. GET /admin/platform-metrics [Token admin required]}
